Nihari Recipe

______________________________________________________________________

Ingredients

It takes 21 ingredients to cook.

  • 1kg Shanks Meat (large pieces)
  • 1kg Bones and Marrow Bones
  • 2 tablespoons Ground Gralic
  • Salt as per taste
  • 2 medium (chopped) Onions
  • 10 to 12 Black peppercones
  • 2 tablespoon Red pepper Powder
  • 2 tablespoon Coriander Powder
  • 1 table spoon White Cumin
  • 1 tablespoon Fennal Seeds
  • 1 teaspoon Cardamom Seeds
  • 1 teaspoon Dried Ginger
  • 1 teaspoon Pipli

    • (It is an ingredient of all spice powder like a grey colored round.)

  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 Lemon
  • 1 cup Oil

    • For Garnishing

  • Thin sliced Ginger
  • Thin sliced green chilies
  • Chopped green coriander
  • Diced lemon

Directions

Step 1

Grind fennel seeds, cardamom seeds, dried ginger and pipli and place aside.Add salt, black peppercorns, red pepper, coriander powder, cumin and recently ground spice to yogurt.Pour oil in a pan and fry for 2 to 3 minutes on medium heat. Then add onions and fry until golden brown.

Step 2

Add garlic to fried onions and fry for 1 to 2 minutes.Add flour and roast unless it releases aroma.Now put spices into pan which you mixed lately with yogurt. Roast until oil comes up. Now add meat and cook well and also add bones and marrow bones.

Step 3

Then add 3 liter water and cook on medium heat (3 to 4 hours) until meat is tenderized. When meat is fully tender and nihari is thick, add lemon juice and cook on low heat for 5 minutes.When oil comes up, nihari is ready.Serve with ginger, green chilies, coriander, lemon and naans.
