Haleem Recipe

______________________________________________________________________

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kg (small pieces) Chicken
  • 1 tsp Ginger Garlic Paste
  • 1 cup Oil
  • 1 Chopped Onion
  • Cinnamon 1 stick
  • 6 pcs Green Cardamom
  • 1/2 cup Curd/Yougurt
  • 2 tbsp Almonds (optional)
  • 1/2 cup Coconut Milk (optional)
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Red chilli powder
  • 1 tsp Whole Coriander
  • 1/2 tsp All spices Powder

Method

Step 1

Heat oil in wok and add onions and ginger garlic paste.Now add whole spices, cinnamon, green cardamom, chicken, salt, red chilli powder and coriander seeds.Add coconut milk, curd and almonds.

Mix well and cook until an oil layer becomes visible.Ad green chillies and all spices powder. Mix well.

Your Chicken Karahi in Pakistani style is ready to be served in just simple easy steps.
